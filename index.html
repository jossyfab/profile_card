Rottweiler

The Rottweiler is a large working breed from Germany, where they are known as Rottweiler Metzgerhund.

Life Expectancy:8-10 years

weight: Female: 35-48 kg, Male: 50-60 kg

Height: Female: 56-63 cm, Male: 61-69 cm

The Rottweiler is a robust working breed of great strength descended from the mastiffs of the Roman legions. A gentle playmate and protector within the family circle, the Rottie observes the outside world with self-assured aloofness.
